About Us

Who are we?

SarKar - Bring the change, is a non-profit public friendly website, where the public can raise complaints on issues such as Potholes, BBMP garbage collection and street lights. Public can raise complaints by providing the location for the higher officials to work on the issue. General public are welcomed to give their donations for the betterment of the society and can also volunteer with organanisations of their interest to contribute the society and become a part for a big change.
